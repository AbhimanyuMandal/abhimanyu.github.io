About

Welcome to my corner of the Internet! I'm Abhimanyu Mandal, a curious and motivated 5th-year student on a journey of exploration and discovery through academic research.


As an undergraduate student majoring in Pharmaceutical Engineering and Technology, I have had the incredible opportunity to dive into the realms of knowledge and inquiry. My academic journey has been shaped by a deep-seated passion for Cancer Genomics, Bioinformatics, and Precision Medicine. This passion has driven me to seek challenges, ask probing questions, and explore the unknown.

If you'd like to connect, collaborate, or have any questions about my work, feel free to reach out to me via email or connect with me on LinkedIn. I'm eager to share my enthusiasm for research and learn from the diverse perspective of the academic community.


Thank you for visiting, and let's continue our academic journey together!

Research Experience

Summer research intern

University of Western Ontario, London, Canada

Developing a Deep Learning model for Drug Repurposing in breast cancer.

May 2023 - August 2023
  • Supervisor: Dr. Pingzhao Hu, Associate Professor
  • Contributions:
    1. 16 tumor single-cell samples from HER2+ and ER+ cell lines were aligned with epithelial cells from normal breast tissues to identify differentially expressed genes in each cell type cluster.
    2. The drug reference generated from the drug response data was used to evaluate the drug response score for each gene in a cluster.
  • Outcome: Identified potential drug combinations, such as Artesunate and Enalapril, to treat breast cancer. These drugs were originally used for treating severe malaria and managing hypertension and congestive heart failures, respectively.
  • Tools/Skills used: R programming, Immunology.

Machine Learning based Neurotoxicity prediction

January 2021 - April 2021
  • Supervisor: Dr. Senthil Raja A., Associate Professor
  • Contributions:
